## Output Format

Action yang available:
1. {"action": "activate", "card": "Nama Kartu", "zone": "hand"}
   — Aktifkan Spell/Trap dari hand, atau aktifkan efek monster
2. {"action": "summon", "card": "Nama Kartu", "position": "attack"}
   — Normal Summon monster (position: attack/defense)
3. {"action": "set", "card": "Nama Kartu", "zone": "spell"}
   — Set monster (zone: monster) atau Spell/Trap (zone: spell)
4. {"action": "attack", "target": "monster_1"}
   — Masuk Battle Phase & attack monster lawan
5. {"action": "direct_attack"}
   — Attack langsung
6. {"action": "end_turn"}
   — End turn
7. {"action": "chain_yes", "card": "Nama Kartu"}
   — Respond chain / activate efek
8. {"action": "chain_no"}
   — Skip chain response
9. {"action": "wait"}
   — Gak ada yang bisa dilakukan, tunggu
